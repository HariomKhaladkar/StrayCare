Throughout the internship period (July 2025 to March 2026), these students have exhibited exceptional dedication and technical proficiency. Our sponsorship included comprehensive technical and non-technical support, ensuring they had the necessary resources, guidance, and expertise to successfully implement their project.

We are pleased to report that the project has met all its objectives and requirements, showcasing innovative solutions and a high level of technical competence. The StrayCare Platform successfully integrates AI-driven stray animal reporting, GIS-based hotspot mapping, smart NGO dispatch, real-time rescue coordination, and an animal food marketplace — all aimed at addressing the critical social issue of stray animal welfare.

The students' work on this project is a testament to their hard work and the effective collaboration between I²IT and ONESNZEROS TECH SOLUTIONS PVT. LTD. We extend our congratulations to the students and trust this project has significantly contributed to their academic and professional development.
